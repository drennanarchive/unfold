Role Evaluation Framework

15-minute decision tool • Reusable for any role

1
What's the ONE thing we need?
Be specific. Not "manage AI strategy." Instead: "Risk assessments before capability deployments" or "Understands the stakeholder ecosystem."
2
Are they good at that?
Track record on this specific task? Relevant skills? Known weakness that blocks them? Be honest about capability vs. potential.
3
How do we make sure they do it?
What structure makes this non-optional? Cadence? Verification? Deadline? How does accountability work given their weakness?
The Verdict
If you can answer those three questions clearly in 15 minutes, you know if the role works.
Everything else is elaboration. The hard question is: Are you willing to structure accountability around their weakness?
